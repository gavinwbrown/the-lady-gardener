Hi, I'm Tracey and did you know that a lady who gardens is known as a gardenerette or a gardengal. A few years ago I decided to leave my office based life behind and become a full time gardener. I have built my business up mostly through word of mouth and I now look after gardens of all sizes, from small town gardens to much larger rural ones. I love the outdoor life, getting close to nature plus the added bonus of a nice cup of tea and maybe a slice of cake. Nothing makes me happier than helping people achieve their dream gardens.
